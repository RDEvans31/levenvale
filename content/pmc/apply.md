# Before You Apply

## What You Need To Know

## Here's what you need to know

**Please read through this carefully to make sure we're a good fit for you!**

### Location

The farm is based just outside **Taunton in Somerset**. Food can be collected directly from the farm, but we also offer **nationwide shipping** for members across the UK.

### Food Access

Membership gives you access to our **private Community Pantry**, where you can contribute further based on what you choose to receive.

- Orders are shipped with next-day delivery, Monday to Thursday
- **Regular collection days** are available if you're local to the farm
- All food is seasonal, produced on-site, and offered in rhythm with the land

### Membership Commitment

- £50/month contribution
- Minimum 12-month commitment
- 90 days' notice required to cancel
- Membership continues beyond 12 months unless you choose otherwise
- Your membership covers your whole household — including your partner and children.

### Farm Visits & Involvement

While not open access, members are invited to attend seasonal gatherings and farm work days throughout the year, as well as our annual **Summer Celebration**. We also have other opportunities to get involved and connect, and you'll be the first to hear as they arise.

### Community Note

**This is a collaborative community — not a traditional service.**

While the club stewards and team are responsible for the **production of food**, we welcome aligned members who want to participate, contribute, and help build something real — together.

## Your Contribution

## Your Contribution

_to the land and the community_

Membership is **£50/month**, and it's a contribution — not a transaction.

That money goes toward:

- Maintaining the land
- Running the community space
- Hosting gatherings
- Covering operational costs so that we can continue to provide for you

Food is accessed through our **completely private, online Community Pantry** — members can tailor their contributions and allocations to suit their household needs.

**A single membership covers your whole household — including your partner and children. When you apply, you'll be asked to list who's included in your household membership.**

## Who You Are
