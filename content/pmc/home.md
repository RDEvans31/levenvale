## About

## Private. Not-for-profit. Non-commercial.

This is not a business. It's not a shop. And it's definitely not for everyone.

This is a private, not-for-profit members club — rooted in land, built in community, and protected from the mainstream system.

By becoming a member, you're not buying a product. You're supporting a living project that produces food, restores land, and nurtures community — and in return, you gain access to:

- Independently produced, nutrient-dense food
- A like-minded community that values freedom, sovereignty, and service
- The land itself — a place where your food is grown, and where you're welcome to come learn, connect, and contribute

## Food

## Our Food

All produce available exclusively to our private members club.

- **Grass-fed Beef** — Ethically raised 100% grass-fed cattle on our regenerative pastures, producing the highest quality beef.
- **Raw Dairy** — 100% Grass-fed raw dairy products, available in the near future from our 100% purebred Jersey cows.
- **Beyond Free-range Pastured Poultry** — Pastured broiler chickens and fresh eggs from our free-range pastured laying hens.
- **Beyond Organic Veg** — No-dig vegetables, seasonal herbs, and fruit grown with regenerative practices.
- **Venison, Pheasant, and Other Wild Game** — Offered when seasonally available, sourced from our local trusted channels.
- **Traditional Foods** — 100% grass-fed tallow, nutrient-dense bone broth, sauerkraut, and traditional preserves.

## What This Isn't

## What This Isn't

If you're looking for supermarket-style convenience — this isn't that.

We care deeply about our commitment to members, and we'll do our best to provide a reliable and diverse supply of food.

But this project is still in its early days. We're still building the foundations.

Our food is produced seasonally, in rhythm with the land, and on a small, regenerative scale.

We are **not certified organic** and never will be because we shouldn't have to pay to produce good food. However, all of our operational inputs meet **organic standards or beyond.**

We won't try to compete with the consistency and uniformity of supermarkets — because that system depends on long supply chains, environmental harm, and disconnection from our natural surroundings.

Instead, we offer something real:

- Food Grown with Care
- Animals Raised with Dignity
- Produce that Reflects the Seasons
